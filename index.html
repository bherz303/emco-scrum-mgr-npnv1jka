Scoring Tiers

5 pts Green — Nailed it. This is working the way it should.
3 pts Yellow — Partially there, but needs attention.
1 pt Red — Not in place. Standard items still earn 1 pt.
0 pts Bonus Red — The 2 bonus criteria earn 0 on Red (no partial credit). All points count toward the same total — a perfect board scores 60.

Eval Modes

📋
Board Hygiene Only — 60 pts total Pulled live from your Monday board. Can run anytime, no meeting required. A perfect board scores 60.
⭐
Full Eval — 100 pts Board Hygiene + 8 Meeting Management items scored during or after a sync.

Board Hygiene Criteria

60 pts total — perfect score possible
1
LOW Values on Today Items
5 pts All Today items have a valid Fibonacci LOW — 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, or 21.
3 pts 1 or 2 Today items are missing a LOW or have an invalid value (like 0 or 4).
1 pt 3 or more Today items have no LOW, or LOWs aren't being used at all.
2
Pulse Titles
5 pts Titles describe the next action and give enough context to know what the task is.
3 pts A few titles are vague or generic.
1 pt Most titles are things like "Follow up," "Call," or "Meeting" with zero context.
3
Later Group Dates
5 pts Every Deferred item has a future Monday-specific due date set.
3 pts 1 or 2 deferred items have no date or a non-Monday date.
1 pt 3 or more deferred items with wrong dates — automations won't fire correctly.

💡 Dates must be Mondays — that's when the automation returns items to your Inbox for review.

4
QG Pulses & Happiness Pulse Visible
5 pts At least one QG Live pulse is visible in Today or This Week, AND a Happiness pulse is present in either group.
3 pts One of the two is present — either QG pulses are visible or a Happiness pulse exists, but not both.
1 pt No QG Live pulses visible in active groups AND no Happiness pulse present.

💡 QG Live pulses parked in Deferred with no date don't count — they need to be in Today or This Week to be visible to the team.

5
QG Value on Quarterly Goal Pulses
5 pts All active QG Live pulses have the QG Value column filled in.
3 pts Some QG Live pulses are missing QG values.
1 pt The QG Value column isn't being used on QG pulses at all.
6
Inbox Age
5 pts No untriaged pulses older than 5 days sitting in the Inbox.
3 pts 1 or 2 Inbox items are older than 5 days.
1 pt 3 or more stale items, or the Inbox is being used as a dumping ground.

💡 Triage means: assign it, set a date, move it — don't let things rot in Inbox.

7
Project Column
5 pts At least half of active pulses have a Project set, AND all QG and DB Pulse items are correctly tagged.
3 pts At least half filled in but a QG or DB Pulse item is missing its tag — OR fewer than half filled in but all QG and DB Pulse items are correct.
1 pt Fewer than half of active pulses have a Project set AND QG or DB Pulse items are missing their tags.

💡 QG (Quarterly Goal) and DB Pulse (Roundtable) items must always be tagged. Everything else — aim for at least half coverage.

8
Updates with Context
5 pts All critical pulses tied to ongoing projects — QG, truck repair or maintenance, customer issues, and employee tracking — have current updates.
3 pts Most critical pulses tied to ongoing projects — QG, sales, trucks, customer issues, and employee tracking — have current updates.
1 pt No pulses have updates or show progress.

💡 Simple task placeholders, LOW tracking items, and damage pulses/claims don't need updates and aren't counted. Focus on QG, fleet, customer, and employee pulses — the ones where progress actually needs to be logged.

9
No Duplicates
5 pts One pulse per task — no duplicate entries cluttering the board.
3 pts 1 or 2 suspected duplicates — same title, same date, same apparent task.
1 pt Clear duplicates present — identical task entered more than once with no differentiation.

💡 Sales blocks are NOT duplicates. Managers run multiple sales blocks per week on different days (Monday PM, Tuesday AM, Thursday AM, etc.) — each one is a separate, intentional pulse.

10
Automations
5 pts Items move via status changes — group distribution matches the automation flow.
3 pts A few irregularities — some items in wrong groups for their status.
1 pt Items are clearly being dragged manually — statuses and groups don't line up.

💡 Use status changes to move pulses. No drag and drop — that breaks the automation logic.

★
Bonus — Definition of Done
5 pts Done group items have a meaningful update explaining what was accomplished.
3 pts Some Done items have notes, others are blank.
0 pts No updates on Done pulses — no partial credit on this one.

💡 Damage pulses/claims are excluded — a closed damage claim without a note isn't counted against you.

★
Bonus — No Past-Due Items
5 pts No items in Today or This Week have a past due date.
3 pts 1 or 2 items are past their due date.
0 pts 3 or more past-due items sitting around — no partial credit.

💡 If it's past due, either complete it, defer it with a new Monday date, or delete it. Don't let it linger.

Meeting Management Criteria

Full Eval only — 40 pts
1
Pre-Sync Board Prep Board is prepped 10–15 minutes before the meeting, and the Regional Manager is alerted when it's ready.
5 ptsBoard prepped on time and RM alerted before the meeting starts.
3 ptsBoard prepped but RM wasn't alerted, or it was prepped late.
1 ptBoard wasn't prepped before the meeting.
2
Agenda Flow Meeting follows the standard order — Velocity check, then Hold for Sync, then This Week, then Today, then Inbox cleanup.
5 ptsFollowed the exact agenda order start to finish.
3 ptsMostly followed but skipped a step or went out of order.
1 ptNo clear structure — meeting jumped around with no agenda.
3
Time Management Each person stays within 10 minutes. The meeting doesn't run long because one topic took over.
5 ptsEveryone stayed within 10 minutes. Meeting finished on time.
3 ptsOne person ran over but the meeting recovered.
1 ptMeeting ran long because time wasn't managed.
4
Tabling Discussions Big topics that derail the flow get tabled and followed up on after the sync — not allowed to eat the meeting.
5 ptsOff-topic items were tabled promptly and captured for follow-up.
3 ptsSome derailment happened but was recovered before major damage.
1 ptA tangent took over the meeting and wasn't redirected.
5
Tone The meeting is engaged and constructive. People are present and adding energy — not checked out or combative.
5 ptsEnergy was high, everyone was present and constructive.
3 ptsMostly engaged but some distraction or low energy.
1 ptPeople checked out or the tone was negative or combative.
6
Manager Release The BM releases FMs and Flex managers early when appropriate so they can get to on-sites or sales calls.
5 ptsFMs and Flex released at the right time without being asked.
3 ptsReleased but late, or only after prompting.
1 ptFMs were held through the whole meeting unnecessarily.
7
Automations in Meeting No drag-and-drop during the sync. Pulses move via status changes only — every time.
5 ptsZero drag-and-drop. Every pulse moved via status change.
3 pts1–2 instances of drag-and-drop caught during the meeting.
1 ptDrag-and-drop used throughout — automation logic is broken.
8
Velocity Reviewed The team's velocity is checked and discussed during the sync — DLOW and WLOW are reviewed and the pace for the week is acknowledged.
5 ptsVelocity was reviewed, discussed, and the week's pace acknowledged.
3 ptsVelocity was mentioned but not meaningfully discussed.
1 ptVelocity wasn't covered at all.

5 Things That Move Your Score the Most

1
Set a LOW on every Today item Before your sync — go through Today and make sure every pulse has a valid Fibonacci number. It takes 2 minutes.
2
Tag the Project column on active pulses This is the most commonly missed item. A quick pass through Today assigning project tags gets you easy points.
3
Keep your Velocity pulse active — don't Done it The Velocity pulse should live in Today or This Week permanently. Marking it Done kills your velocity tracking.
4
Always defer to a Monday date When you defer something, set the date to the Monday you plan to revisit it. Any other day breaks the automation.
5
Post a brief update on active pulses Even one sentence — "Waiting on parts," "Called client, no answer" — makes the board tell the story of your work.
💡
Target DLOW of 15–20 Daily Level of Work should be 15 to 20 per person. If Today is overloaded, triage — move lower-priority items to This Week or defer them.
